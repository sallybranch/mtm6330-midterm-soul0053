At Sallys Rabbit Haven we strive to be the home for abandoned rabbits and to grant them new homes where they can receive love, affection, and attention. We bring in rabbits all across the Ottawa county with some exceptions to outside of Ottawa.
Please take a look at our available adoptions if you're interested in bringing home a new part of your family. Or check out how to maintain a rabbits life in our rabbit care page.
